Invisible Architecture: Gig Health in Nigeria

Health is now a "digital byproduct." For 12.2 million workers, algorithms are the primary social determinants of health, setting sleep cycles and stress levels through UX design.

The Stress-Inflation Loop

As Naira devalues, screen time must increase to maintain purchasing power, leading to permanent cortisol spikes.

Triple Fatigue

The mental load of the task + algorithmic surveillance + physical anxiety of power/data fluctuations.

Atomization of Labor

Shift from communal African work structures to solitary, solitary digital isolation (equivalent to chronic smoking).
